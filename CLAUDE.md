## Style

<!--
Start with whatever stylebook you actually use, then add house rules.
The defaults below are AP plus a few common newsroom conventions.
Delete what doesn't apply, add what does.
-->

- AP style for all writing
- No Oxford comma
- Spell out numbers under 10, use figures for 10 and above
- Dollar amounts: $2.3 million, not $2,300,000
- Sentence-case headlines

## Source standards

<!--
This is where you tell Claude how you handle attribution, anonymous
sources, and verification. Be opinionated. Newsrooms differ on this and
Claude should be matching your house, not a generic average.
-->

- Attribute every claim to a named source unless there's a strong reason not to
- Flag unverified claims separately from verified ones
- For dollar amounts, vote counts, and dates, note whether the figure can be checked against a primary document
